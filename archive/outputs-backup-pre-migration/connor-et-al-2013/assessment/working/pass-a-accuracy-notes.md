## Claims Assessment (76 items)

### Overall: GOOD (94.7%)

**Errors Identified:** 4 errors affecting accuracy

#### Error C001 - Under-extraction
- **Severity:** Minor (-0.5 points)
- **Issue:** Claim doesn't capture the temporal sequencing emphasis from verbatim quote
- **Current:** "Vegetation shifted from cold steppe to semidesert to forest-steppe to mixed oak woods, followed by deforestation"
- **Verbatim (p.3):** "Our record indicates the persistence of cold steppe vegetation from ~37,500 to 17,900 cal. a BP, semidesert vegetation from ~17,900 to 10,300 cal. a BP..."
- **Note:** The claim accurately summarises but loses the precise dating that makes this an empirical finding rather than generic statement
- **Not Critical:** Dates are preserved in supporting evidence items

#### Error C013 - Missing Context
- **Severity:** Moderate (-2.0 points)
- **Issue:** Claim states timing without comparative context that makes this finding significant
- **Current:** "The first agricultural settlements in Bulgarian Thrace date to around 8000 cal. a BP"
- **Missing Context:** This date is significant because it represents a ~3000-year delay after agriculture appeared in nearby Anatolia (~11,000 cal BP). The paper's argument centres on explaining this delay.
- **Impact:** Claim appears as simple chronological statement rather than evidence for delayed agricultural expansion
- **Recommendation:** Add claim nature as "comparative" or reference the delay explicitly

#### Error C063 - Duplicate Content
- **Severity:** Minor (-0.5 points)
- **Issue:** C063 content duplicated with E056 content
- **C063:** "Age-depth model constructed using Markov chain Monte-Carlo analysis with Bayesian statistical approach in OxCal 4.1.7"
- **E056:** "Age-depth model constructed using Markov chain Monte-Carlo analysis with Bayesian statistical approach in OxCal 4.1.7 based on IntCal09 calibration curve..."
- **Note:** E056 has rationalisation note "Consolidated from E056, E057, C063 in Pass 2"
- **Impact:** C063 should have been removed during Pass 2 consolidation but remains as separate claim
- **Recommendation:** Remove C063 or transform into interpretive claim about methodological appropriateness

#### Error C096 - Misattribution
- **Severity:** Moderate (-2.0 points)
- **Issue:** Claim presents methodological principle from literature as if it's a finding from this paper
- **Current claim_type:** "methodological"
- **Current content:** "Pollen source-area of large sites (>100 ha) is dominated by regional pollen component, representing spatial area of ~10^4-10^5 km^2"
- **Verbatim (p.13):** "The second consideration is that the pollen source-area of large sites (>100 ha) is dominated by a regional pollen component (Jacobson and Bradshaw, 1981; Sugita, 2007)."
- **Issue:** This is established theory being applied, not a methodological finding from Connor et al. Should be theoretical/literature claim or incorporated into method description
- **Recommendation:** Change claim_type to "theoretical" or remove as claim and add to relevant method
