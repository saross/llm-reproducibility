## Contextual Interpretation

### Robustness (50, Moderate)

**Why this score:** Abductive research involves inference to the best explanation rather than systematic testing of alternatives. The paper considers alternative interpretations but does not exhaustively test them through triangulation or sensitivity analysis. This is a characteristic of interpretive humanities methodology, not a deficiency.

**What this means:** The interpretation is coherent and well-argued but depends on the oral tradition theoretical framework. Readers accepting different frameworks might reach different conclusions from the same evidence.

**What readers should consider:** Evaluate whether the oral tradition dating model is convincing. Consider alternative explanations for asymmetric language representation in Homer (e.g., narrative convention, poetic tradition).

### Reproducibility (55, Moderate) ðŸ”§
