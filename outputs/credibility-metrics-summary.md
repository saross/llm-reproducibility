## Metric Definitions

**ESD (Evidential Support Density):** Claims:Evidence ratio. Lower is better (more evidence per claim).

**TCI (Transparency Completeness Index):** RDMAP coverage (0-1 scale). Higher is better.

**SCS (Scope Constraint Score):** Count of limitation statements. Higher is better.

**RTI (Robustness Triangulation Index):** Shannon diversity of evidence types (0-3 typical). Higher is better.

**RIS (Replicability Infrastructure Score):** PIDs and sharing statements (0-10 scale). Higher is better.

**PGCS (PID Graph Connectivity Score):** Connectivity between PIDs. Higher is better.

**FCS (FAIR Compliance Score):** Aggregate FAIR assessment (0-15 scale). Higher is better.

**MDD (Methods Documentation Density):** Mean characters per RDMAP item. Higher is better.
