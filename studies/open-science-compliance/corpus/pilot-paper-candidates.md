# Phase 1 Pilot Paper Candidates

**Status:** Shortlist for collaborative selection
**Target:** 5 papers for Phase 1 Exploratory Pilot
**Journal:** Journal of Archaeological Science (JAS)
**Date compiled:** 2025-01-09

## Selection Criteria Applied

Papers were identified using:

1. **Publication window:** 2023-2025 (post-JAS reproducibility policy)
2. **Computational component:** Code and/or data mentioned
3. **Open access:** Gold OA or accessible via repository
4. **Repositories consulted:**
   - [benmarwick/ctv-archaeology](https://github.com/benmarwick/ctv-archaeology) - CRAN Task View
   - [apalmisano82/Open_Science](https://github.com/apalmisano82/Open_Science) - Open science practices dataset
   - Zenodo, GitHub, and OSF searches

## Sampling Strategy Recommendation

For Phase 1 (5 papers), recommend selecting:

| Category | Papers | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| **Confirmed exemplary** | 2 | Crema 2024 (#1), Marwick 2025 (#2) — strong compendia |
| **Award-recognised** | 1 | Herskind 2024 (#4) — JAS-SAS award |
| **Method diversity** | 2 | Key 2024 (#3) lithics + Dye 2023 (#5) chronology |

This provides:
- Mix of data types (radiocarbon, morphometric, linguistic, chronological)
- Mix of statistical approaches (Bayesian, ML/NLP, linear estimation)
- Papers recognised for reproducibility/quality
- Post-policy publications (2023-2024)
